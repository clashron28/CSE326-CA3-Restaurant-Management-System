Fish Curry

Fish Curry is a delightful seafood dish, known for its bold flavors and aromatic spices. Fresh fish fillets are simmered in a tangy gravy made from onions, tomatoes, and tamarind, enriched with coconut milk or yogurt for a smooth texture. Infused with spices like turmeric, mustard seeds, cumin, and curry leaves, the dish strikes a perfect balance between heat and acidity. Regional variations of fish curry exist across India, from the fiery Goan curry to the coconut-laden Kerala version. Best enjoyed with steamed rice, this curry offers a burst of coastal flavors in every bite.
